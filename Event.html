DRONE LEAGUE

Drone League is a thrilling and dynamic competition that merges the realms of hardware engineering, software programming, and strategic thinking. Participants are tasked with designing and programming autonomous drones capable of navigating a specified course, avoiding obstacles, and completing the race in the shortest possible time. This event challenges participants to create robust algorithms for precise control, obstacle detection, and dynamic pathfinding, adapting to various course complexities and environmental factors. It’s perfect for those passionate about advancing in the field of autonomous aerial systems and pushing the boundaries of drone technology.

Line follower is a dynamic and engaging competition that blends hardware design, software development, and problem-solving skills.
